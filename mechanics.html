01

Core Rules

// prime directiveV is a game about endstage capitalism and the dystopia it creates. Combat is the primary mode of interaction. All rules serve the spirit of the game ‚Äî when ambiguity arises, consult a marshal. Their call is final.

The Absolute Basics

Combat in V is silent ‚Äî basic attacks require no verbal call. Players wield Nerf blasters and foam melee weapons. All damage is equalized regardless of weapon type.

// rule zero1 HIT = 1 DAMAGE ‚Äî always, everywhere.

In-Play vs. Out-of-Play

The default state at any V event is in-play ‚Äî you are your character. Out-of-play is indicated by wearing a neon OOP sash, or crossing index and middle fingers against your forehead briefly. Holding long OOP conversations near in-play players is a violation of gang vibe.

Pause, Action & Emergency

PAUSE ‚Äî All action stops. Players freeze in place. Marshal call only.

ACTION ‚Äî Play resumes after a Pause. Marshal call only.

EMERGENCY ‚Äî Real-life safety crisis. Everyone drops to OOP. No exceptions.

Consent & Safety Signals

Cross index and middle fingers over the heart to signal discomfort. All action suspends immediately, no questions asked.

Say "Infirmary" to a marshal or NPC to exit a scene safely. They respond "Granted" and suspend action.

Searching

Searching is never physical. Stand next to a downed/restrained target and declare "Searching." Target relinquishes all in-game items. Represents a full search of clothing, bags, and containers.

Honor System

V operates on a three-strike system. First: verbal warning. Second: formal written warning requiring signature. Third: suspension up to full membership revocation.

Combat System

HEAD & GROIN
= ILLEGAL TARGETS
Barriers (2)
Absorb first 2 hits to your whole body. Regenerate 1 every 15 consecutive seconds without damage. Does not stop special taglines.
Graze
First hit past barriers on any zone. Must be actrepped ‚Äî yelp, recoil, grab the area. Shake it off and keep fighting.
Wound
Second hit on a zone. Zone is out of action. Arms: one-handed only. Legs: limp. Torso: drop prone, bleed count begins.
Bleed / INCAP / COLD
First wound starts a 5-min bleed count (+1 min per additional wound). INCAP: unconscious, kill rag on head. COLD: death state.

Actrep Requirements

V combat is theatrical. Grazes, wounds, fire, electricity, knockout ‚Äî all must be actrepped convincingly. Failure to actrep is considered cheating. Melee swings are performed at medium touch speed at a 45-degree angle from the body.

Combat Reset

A marshal may call a "Combat Reset" during long engagements. Instantly refreshes all action slots, restores wounds, and refreshes barriers. Players in INCAP or COLD are not restored by this call.

Cover Objects

Inflatable cover cannot be moved by one player ‚Äî takes two to drag at walking pace. Red-colored objects contain explosives and call SPLASH 10 BOOM IGNITE if struck by bullets or explosives.

Item Drops

NPCs may stand within ~10 seconds and respawn. If holding a key item, they hold it up then place it on the ground. Pick it up like a video game item drop.

06

Safety Protocols

Eye Protection

Mandatory at all times in any "weapons free" area. Must be at minimum wraparound style. May only be removed in designated sanctuary/weapons-down zones.

Trigger Discipline

Maintain proper trigger discipline at all times. Discharging a weapon within a weapons-down zone ‚Äî intentional or negligent ‚Äî results in immediate corrective action with in-play and potentially out-of-play consequences.

Cover & Blind Firing

When firing from behind cover, always present a legal target. Do not blind fire or peek only your head. If you're going to lean out ‚Äî lean out properly.

Physical Contact

Requires verbal consent: "Physical Roleplay ‚Äî may I [X]?" Recipient must respond "I Accept." Grappling, wrestling, or striking in combat is strictly forbidden.

Age & Conduct

Players must be 18+ with valid ID at first check-in. Alcohol, drugs, and intoxicating substances are strictly forbidden on-site at any time.

Weapon Physrep Rules

All weapon physreps must be painted to eliminate garish colors and logos. Orange barrels may remain unpainted. Melee physreps must match their real-world counterpart aesthetically ‚Äî no basic boffer sticks.

Theft Policy

Stealing any out-of-play item is strictly forbidden. In-play theft requires a marshal present; the physrep is turned over to the marshal and replaced with a card.

// noteIn-play theft from allies violates gang culture and invites in-play disciplinary action.

Emotional Safety

Cross index and middle fingers over the heart to exit a scene silently. Say "Infirmary" to a marshal ‚Äî no questions asked, no consequences. Emotional safety staff are available at all events.

07

Combat Taglines

Taglines are verbal calls that modify basic combat. Splash [#] = area-of-effect radius in feet. Pure bypasses all barriers and armor.

Full
Target takes 1 point of damage to every body zone simultaneously.
Shred
Like Full, but delivered via melee. Strips all zones at once with a weapon strike.
Splash [#]
Area-of-effect. All targets within the stated foot radius are affected by the following tagline.
Boom
Explosion effect. Destroys cover objects and structures it strikes. Typically delivered with Splash.
Ignite
Target is set on fire. Must be fully actrepped ‚Äî screaming, dropping, arm-waving.
Concuss
Target is stunned by percussive force. Must actrep disorientation and stagger.
Knockout
Target is rendered immediately unconscious and must drop to INCAP state.
Knockdown / Knockback
Target knocked to the ground (Knockdown) or pushed back several feet (Knockback). Must actrep.
Disarm
Target must drop their currently held weapon. Item goes to ground.
Blind
Target must close their eyes and actrep blindness. Duration as specified by ability.
Mute
Target cannot speak or vocalize for the duration. Cannot use abilities that require calling out.
Silence
Target cannot use any abilities or skills for 5 seconds. Base weapons and movement only.
Pure
Bypasses all barriers and armor points. Hits zones directly. Hardest damage type to defend against.
Restore
Reverses damage on a targeted zone, restoring it to normal function. Can also restore broken objects.
Resist
All-purpose defensive negation. The intended attack or effect is completely nullified.
Break
Renders physical items (weapons, shields, doors, cover) inoperative. Also cancels active status taglines.
Execute
Moves a downed target (wounded torso) immediately to INCAP. Requires point-blank stance with both feet planted. Melee = 3-second count.
Death
Target goes immediately to COLD state. Bypasses all stages of damage and bleed. Rare, high-cost ability.
Devastate
Target immediately takes 6 points of damage to all zones. Cover and objects are destroyed as with Break.
Corrode
A specialized damage effect delivered via blade or bullet. Used by Venom gang members at level 4.
Shock
Electrical damage effect used by Vesper netrunners. Actrep convulsions/electrical impact.
Overdrive
Steeljaw-exclusive. An incredibly fast attack ‚Äî lands 2 damage before modifiers on one target.
Armor Piercing / Piercing
Bypasses Armor or AP. Does not bypass barriers. Can be resisted by skills.
Reveal
Cancels all stealth within the given radius. Affected players cannot re-enter stealth for 10 seconds.
Compel
Control a target's cyberware. Commands: "Stop," "Run away," or "Fight your friends." Cannot target enemies in Netspace.
Indicate
A non-line-of-sight attack (ricochet, remote hack, etc.). Marshal-mediated.
Clank
Indicates armor absorbing damage. Used by Iron Titans with shields or units with Armor abilities.
Incoming
Marshal-use only. Warns of heavy ordnance ‚Äî falling bombs, distant artillery. Environmental hazards follow.
Backlash
NPC-use only. Indicate-type tagline for anyone backpacking. The backpacker takes the effect; the meatspace ally does not.
// reminderBasic attacks (1 hit = 1 damage) require NO verbal call. Taglines are only spoken when activating a special ability or modified effect. Keep the field clean and calls clear.
